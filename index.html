Long Term Local

Byron bay is a special place and I'm proud to have called my self part of the unique community here for over 30 years. This passion for the region comes across in my music. If you love this area enough to make it your location for your important day then it makes sense to book someone who share a similar connection to provide the music.

Years Of Experience

Ive been djing for over 20 years, and in that time have played hundreds of weddings. All of them unique. No matter what style of music, or what theme, you can be sure that you will be in safe hands.

Versatile

Whatever your musical preference, you can be sure I can provide. I'm also more than simply a wedding dj. I've hosted and dj'd at many charity fundraisers for our local radio station (Bay Fm), as well as having a show on there for many years. I've also hosted and MC'd a wide range events from blue ticket events like the Blues Fest to smaller more intimate affairs.

More than just a dj...

All dj bookings include a sit-down consultation where we discuss in detail what you want for your big day. This includes things like choice of music, run times e.t.c. Pad this out with more info Beav...
