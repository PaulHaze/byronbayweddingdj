﻿ Greetings and G'day from beautiful Byron Bay﻿

My name is Beaver, and my friends call me Beave. Music is my l﻿ife. Having been a community radio presenter on Bay FM for 18 years, I am well-known for my music and sourced for many weddings and events. I feel privileged to share in such a special occasion and I enjoy my work immensely.

My usual technique involves meeting with clients’ prior, enabling us to become familiar over about 30 minutes and to flick through my box of tunes. This eases all minds regarding the soundscape and dance floor we wish to create.

Upon commencement, light jazz blended with well-produced cafe beats will be complemented with hints of the greats such as Nina Simone, Frank Sinatra, Ella Fitzgerald and Louie Armstrong, through to more recent artists such as Norah Jones and Jack Johnson.

We can then build a dance floor choosing retro disco, funk and soul, and music people know and are comfortable having a shake to. On occasions clients have asked for specifics, such as house music or other dance music, which is all possible depending on your personal musical taste. Cultural themes such as South American and Cuban flavours do tend to complement the day as well.

I have been playing music for over 20 years across a wide spectrum of events and musical themes — Bay FM street parties, Byron’s infamous house parties, birthday celebrations, corporate events, Christmas parties, New Years Eve parties, and my speciality, weddings. I bring all of this experience to your special occasion. 

All is possible and the music shall hold fond memory. I am happy to play from when your guests arrive for your ceremony until they leave your celebration.

If you would like to email me with your enquiry, I am more than happy to respond.

Regards,
Beaver
